Purpose

This directory contains the examples and applications to demonstrate the capabilities of the B-WB1M-WPAN1.

Most of those projects are generated with STM32CubeMX tool to initialize the system, peripherals and middleware stacks.

Several applications are provided ready to use with IAR©, KEIL© and STM32CubeIDE© to demonstrate the Bluetooth Low Energy capabilities of the device.
